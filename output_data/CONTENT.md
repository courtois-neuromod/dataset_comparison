# Output Data

Generated by `invoke run`:

- `datasets_tidy_per_subject.csv` — tidy long-format table (per-subject unique fields) produced by `make_tables`; columns: `dataset`, `group`, `group_color`, `modality`, `dotpath`, `unit`, `value`.
- `datasets_tidy_total.csv` — tidy long-format table (total unique fields) produced by `make_tables`; same columns.
- `dataset_comparison_per_subject.png` — bubble chart of per-subject unique data volume, produced by `summary.ipynb`.
- `dataset_comparison_total.png` — bubble chart of total unique data volume, produced by `summary.ipynb`.
- `dataset_comparison_legend.png` — bubble size legend, produced by `summary.ipynb`.

- `cneuromod_citations.csv` — tidy table of CNeuroMod citation counts; columns: `year`, `type`, `count`; produced by `run-cneuromod-citations`.
- `cneuromod_citations.png` — stacked bar chart of CNeuroMod citations by year and publication type, produced by `cneuromod_citations.ipynb`.

See `source_data/CONTENT.md` for inputs.
